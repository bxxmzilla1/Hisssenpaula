# **App Name**: HISEN

## Core Features:

- Homepage: Display the organization's mission and values prominently.
- Blog: Publish blog posts related to the organization's activities and areas of focus.
- Services Directory: Maintain a directory of available services with descriptions and contact information.
- Events Calendar: Display a calendar of upcoming events with details and registration options.
- Testimonials: Display a section to display success stories from people who used our services
- FAQ Section: Answering some frequently asked question that new people usually have.
- Sponsors Area: Recognize and feature the sponsors who support the organization.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Warm orange (#D4A373), reflecting themes of community and autumn.
- Background color: Dark green (#2E4A35), providing a grounding contrast and natural feel.
- Accent color: Earthy yellow (#BDB24D) for calls to action and highlighting important content.
- Headline font: 'Playfair' for titles, providing a elegant, high-end feel. Body font: 'PT Sans' for body, modern humanist san-serif.
- Use nature-inspired icons (leaves, trees) to represent growth and community.
- Employ a full-width header image to showcase the organization's impact and vision.
- Incorporate subtle fade-in animations for sections as the user scrolls, enhancing engagement.
